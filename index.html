profile

I'm an '80s girl, born and raised in the mountains of Italy, in a little village near the French border. I moved to the U.S. about 12 years ago and I've lived mostly in Chicago, but also in L.A. and the East Coast. Last year, my partner and I moved to St Louis, MO where we bought our first house. I love it here! I've worked in the foodservice industry for a long time and prior to that I was a project manager in the enviromental sustainability sector. The Covid-19 pandemic changed my industry a lot - and for the worse, unfortunately - so I decided to launch my new career in tech and dove into the Flatiron Software Engineering program.
